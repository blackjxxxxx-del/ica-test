About the Conference

ICA Regional Hub Thailand 2026 is the official concurrent event of the 76th Annual Conference of the International Communication Association—placing Asian perspectives at the center of global communication debates.

Hosted at Chulalongkorn University in Bangkok on 6–7 June 2026, the Hub convenes 200 scholars, practitioners, and policymakers to examine communication inequalities through a regional lens and chart collaborative pathways forward.
