Ethics & Integrity

Publication Ethics and Malpractice Statement

The ICA Regional Hub Thailand 2026 Proceedings is committed to maintaining the highest standards of academic integrity, transparency, and ethical publication practices.

Responsibilities of Authors +

Originality and Prior Publication
Authors must submit original work that has not been previously published in substantially the same form. Any overlap with previously published work must be clearly disclosed.

Plagiarism and Proper Citation
All sources, quotations, ideas, data, images, and materials from others must be properly cited. Plagiarism, including self-plagiarism and unattributed AI outputs, is strictly prohibited.

Data Integrity and Research Ethics
Fabrication, falsification, manipulation, or misrepresentation of data, findings, methods, or sources is a serious breach of academic ethics.

Authorship
All listed authors must have made a significant contribution to the research and approve the final version submitted.

Use of Artificial Intelligence
AI tools may assist with language editing or formatting. However, authors remain fully responsible for all submitted content. AI systems may not be listed as authors.

Responsibilities of Reviewers +

Reviewers should accept assignments only when they have appropriate expertise and no conflict of interest. All manuscripts must be treated as strictly confidential. Reviews should be conducted objectively, fairly, and professionally, focusing on scholarly quality and relevance. Reviewers must complete evaluations within the designated timeframe and promptly notify the Editorial Team of any anticipated delays.

Responsibilities of Editors and the Proceedings Committee +

Fair Evaluation
Submissions will be assessed solely on academic merit, originality, relevance, and contribution to knowledge — without discrimination based on nationality, gender, institutional affiliation, or any other personal characteristics.

Confidentiality
Editors and Proceedings Committee members shall maintain confidentiality of all submitted manuscripts, review reports, and editorial communications.

Editorial Independence
Editorial decisions shall be made independently and based on scholarly considerations. Acceptance for conference presentation does not guarantee publication in the proceedings.

Ethical Violations and Misconduct +

Allegations of plagiarism, data fabrication, duplicate publication, authorship disputes, or other misconduct will be investigated. Actions may include:

  • Rejection or withdrawal of the submission
  • Retraction of a published paper
  • Notification to the author's institution
  • Exclusion from future ICA Regional Hub Thailand publication opportunities
Important Note on Conference Affiliation

All published papers must acknowledge that they were presented at and reviewed through ICA Regional Hub Thailand 2026, an official concurrent regional event of the International Communication Association (ICA).

Papers published in these proceedings should not be described as papers accepted to or presented at the ICA Annual Conference unless separate acceptance has been obtained through the ICA Annual Conference submission process.
