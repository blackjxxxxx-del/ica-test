Call for Abstracts

ICA-TH 2026 invites scholars, researchers, and practitioners to submit original

research addressing communication inequalities and emerging media dynamics.

🔒 Submission Closed

Abstract Submission Closed

Abstract submission for ICA Regional Hub Thailand 2026 has now officially closed.

The review process is currently underway, and notification of results will be announced before 15 May 2026.

Thank you very much for your interest and submissions.

Submission Overview

The ICA Regional Hub Thailand 2026 welcomes submissions from scholars (including graduate students) and practitioners across Thailand, the Mekong region, and Asia. We invite researchers, policymakers, and civil society professionals to contribute evidence-based insights on communication inequalities within regional contexts.

Submission Types Accepted

Individual Abstract — For oral/virtual presentation in a thematic panel. Reviewed and assigned to a track by the programme committee.
Poster Submission — For visual presentation during dedicated poster sessions. Particularly suitable for work-in-progress and methodological innovations.
Full Paper (Optional) — Available for authors of accepted abstracts seeking publication opportunities or award consideration.

Submission Guidelines

Abstract length: 350–400 words
Submissions must present original research
Authors should clearly identify research questions, methods, and expected contributions
All abstracts must be submitted in English
Each author may submit a maximum of two abstracts
Accepted presenters must register for the conference
