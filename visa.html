Visa Information

Everything international delegates need to know about entering Thailand for ICA-TH 2026, including visa requirements, invitation letters, and arrival tips.

⏱️
Apply Early. Thai visa processing times vary by country and embassy. We strongly recommend applying at least 6–8 weeks before the conference (i.e. by mid-April 2026 at the latest). Do not wait until after abstract acceptance to begin your visa process.

Do You Need a Visa?

Thailand offers visa-free or visa-on-arrival entry to citizens of many countries. Check your eligibility before applying for a full tourist or conference visa.

1

Check Your Eligibility

Visit the official Thai e-Visa portal at thaievisa.go.th to check whether your country requires a visa, is eligible for visa-on-arrival, or qualifies for visa-free entry.

2

Apply Online or at Embassy

If a visa is required, apply through the Thai e-Visa system online or visit your nearest Royal Thai Embassy or Consulate. Conference delegates typically apply for a Tourist Visa (TR) or Non-Immigrant Visa.

3

Request an Invitation Letter

An official invitation letter from ICA-TH 2026 is available to all confirmed registered delegates. Request yours after completing registration — see below for details.

Official Invitation Letter

A formal invitation letter on official letterhead is available to all delegates who have completed registration. This letter supports your visa application.

What the Letter Includes

The official invitation letter confirms your status as a registered delegate or presenter at ICA-TH 2026. It includes conference details, your name, registration status, and the organiser's institutional information.

This letter is for visa application support purposes only and does not constitute a guarantee of visa approval. Final decisions rest entirely with Thai immigration authorities.

  • Your full name and nationality
  • Conference name, dates, and venue
  • Your registration category (presenter / delegate)
  • Organiser name and institutional affiliation
  • Official signature and institutional stamp
📩

How to Request Your Letter

Invitation letters are issued after confirmed registration. To request yours, email us with your registration confirmation.

Step 1: Complete your registration at the registration page

Step 2: Email info@icathailandhub.org with subject line: "Invitation Letter Request – [Your Full Name]"

Step 3: Include your registration confirmation number

Processing time: 3–5 business days
